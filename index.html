Welcome to the character sorter for Liber Entertainment's game, A3! Act! Addict! Actors!

INSTRUCTIONS
Choose which categories you would like to include for sorting.
After starting, simply click the character that you prefer among the two shown, or, if you equally like/dislike/don't care/don't know about both characters, you can select the "tie" option.
Once all the matches have been solved (or tied), a list of your favorite characters will be automatically generated.

ATTENTION
Please note that this sorter may have spoilers present.

If you would like to sort only the four main troupes, you can check one of the Main Troupe to do so depending if you would like to include the rookies (Chikage, Kumon, Azami, Guy) or not.
Note: Having both Act 1 and Act 2 checked will still include all 24 members of the Main Troupe of Mankai Company.

You can also choose "Characters in MANKAI STAGE" to sort the characters that appear in MANKAI STAGE (From Spring & Summer 2018 up until Four Season LIVE 2020. (Feel free to either sort them for their adaptation from the stage or the game. It's up to your own choice!)

The spoiler characters will only include characters with sprite images in the main/event stories. Some characters may be missing.
If there are missing characters that have sprites are missing, please feel free to send an ask along with the story they are in.

To view the full list of characters in the sorter, click here (warning: there will be spoilers).
This sorter will get updated if new sprites are found through main/event stories.
